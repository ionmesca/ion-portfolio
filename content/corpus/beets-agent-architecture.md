Beets is Ion's AI-native household food operating system. The product includes a cookbook, calendar-native meal planner, persistent shopping list, pantry, household profile, OpenAPI, CLI, and MCP. The portfolio records 442 commits in 30 days, 34 MCP tools designed, and 66 OpenAPI paths. The design thesis is that the agent writes and the human cooks: AI agents can operate planning, importing, and syncing flows while human-owned fields like cooking feedback and household decisions remain protected.
