AI Document Auditor shipped in May 2025. The portfolio records 100+ enterprise customer organizations served, a 67% AI-output retention rate, and 11+ consecutive months of strong adoption. The product uses Gemini-powered equity-document extraction and audits documents against cap-table data. Ion's design focus was trust calibration: classification with judgment rather than pretending every extraction result is equally reliable.
